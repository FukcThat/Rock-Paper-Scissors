Rock - Paper - Scissors

Rules

  • Rock crushes scissors
  • If you choose rock and your opponent chooses scissors, rock wins because it can crush scissors
  • Scissors cuts paper
  • If you choose scissors and your opponent chooses paper, scissors win by cutting the paper
  • Paper covers rock
  • If you choose paper and your opponent chooses rock, paper wins by covering the rock
Singe Round: You play exactly one Round. If it's a tie, there is no winner but the game is over.
Best of 5: You play until either Player reaches 3 wins. If you win one round you get one Point. If it's a tie, neither player gets a point. Ties don't count into the 5 rounds so don't be surprised if the game lasts longer than 5 rounds.
